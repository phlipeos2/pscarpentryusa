Who We Are

Built on a Simple
Belief

PS Carpentry was built on a simple belief: finish work should never be an afterthought.

For over five years, we've partnered with homeowners, general contractors, and interior designers across Utah to deliver the kind of craftsmanship that makes a house feel like a home — and a commercial space feel like a statement.

We specialize exclusively in finish carpentry, which means every member of our team lives and breathes trim, ceilings, stairs, and custom woodwork. We don't spread ourselves thin. We go deep.

When you work with PS Carpentry, you get more than a crew. You get a team that takes ownership of your project from the first measurement to the final walkthrough.
