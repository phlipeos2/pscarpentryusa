What We Do

Finish Carpentry, Done Right

From the first trim to the final detail, we handle every element of finish carpentry with precision and care.

Ceiling & Beam Work

Coffered ceilings, wood panel ceilings, exposed beams, LED integration — architectural details that define a space.

Doors & Hardware

Interior door installation, barn doors, arched entries, and premium hardware fitting — residential and commercial.

Stair Trim & Railings

Custom handrails, floating stairs, wood treads, glass panels, and balustrades built to impress and built to last.

Trim & Molding

Baseboards, crown molding, window casing, door casing, and wainscoting — precision down to the last inch.

Accent Walls

Shiplap, wood slat panels, wainscoting, and fully custom feature walls that transform any room into a statement.

Cabinets & Built-ins

Kitchen cabinet installation, closet systems, bookshelves, and fully custom built-ins designed for your exact space.
