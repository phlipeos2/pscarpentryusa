What We Do

Finish Carpentry,
Done Right.

We specialize exclusively in finish carpentry — every nail, every joint, every detail executed with intention. Here's what we can do for your space.

01
Ceiling & Beam Work

Transform Your Ceiling Into a Statement

Ceilings are one of the most overlooked surfaces in a room — and one of the most impactful. Whether you're after exposed wood beams, coffered panels, shiplap, or decorative tray ceilings, we turn overhead space into a design element that commands attention.

  • Faux wood beams & structural beam wraps
  • Coffered & tray ceilings
  • Shiplap & tongue-and-groove panels
  • Box beam ceiling grids
  • Decorative crown molding at ceiling line

02
Doors & Hardware

First Impressions Start at the Door

A door is the handshake between rooms. We handle everything from interior door installation and casing to hardware placement that feels perfectly balanced. Precision fitting, clean reveals, and flawless alignment — no gaps, no rattles, no excuses.

  • Interior door installation & prehung doors
  • Custom door casing & architrave
  • Barn door & sliding door systems
  • Hardware installation (hinges, handles, closers)
  • Door jamb repair & replacement
Request a Quote
03
Stair Trim & Railings

Staircases That Stop You in Your Tracks

Stairs are the centerpiece of many homes — and every detail matters. We install and trim staircases that blend beauty with structural precision. From classic wood balustrades to modern iron spindles, we work with your vision and deliver a finished result that looks like it belongs in a magazine.

  • Stair tread & riser installation
  • Newel posts & balustrades
  • Wood & iron spindle systems
  • Handrail installation & returns
  • Skirt board & stringer trim

04
Trim & Molding

The Detail That Elevates Every Room

Trim is what separates a finished space from a polished one. Crisp baseboards, layered crown molding, chair rails, wainscoting — we install every profile with tight miters, flush transitions, and zero gaps. The kind of work that makes painters happy and homeowners proud.

  • Baseboard & base cap installation
  • Crown molding (single & stacked profiles)
  • Chair rail & picture rail
  • Wainscoting & paneled walls
  • Window & door casing
Request a Quote
05
Accent Walls

One Wall. Infinite Impact.

Accent walls are the fastest way to transform the personality of a room. We build board-and-batten grids, shiplap features, geometric wood panels, and custom millwork designs that turn a plain wall into a defining moment. Each one is designed and built specifically for your space.

  • Board & batten accent walls
  • Shiplap & V-groove panel walls
  • Geometric wood grid patterns
  • Fireplace surround millwork
  • Feature wall panels for commercial spaces

06
Cabinets & Built-ins

Storage That Looks Like It Was Always There

Custom built-ins feel like architecture — they belong to the room. We install and trim cabinetry, entertainment centers, mud room lockers, pantries, and office built-ins that fit your exact dimensions, match your home's style, and are built to last. No off-the-shelf compromises.

  • Built-in bookshelves & entertainment centers
  • Mud room & laundry room lockers
  • Kitchen cabinet installation & trim-out
  • Home office built-in desks & shelving
  • Custom pantry organization systems
